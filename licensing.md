# Licensing and Attribution

BaudBound is source available for noncommercial use and welcomes community contributions. It is not open source under the Open Source Initiative definition because its software license restricts commercial use.

The license does not grant commercial-use permission. Contact the project owner before using BaudBound as part of a commercial product, paid service, business operation, or other use that may fall outside the permitted purposes.

Original wiki content, the repository README, project screenshots and diagrams, and original non-code website content are provided under [CC BY-NC-SA 4.0](https://github.com/NATroutter/BaudBound/blob/master/CONTENT-LICENSE.md).

You may share and adapt covered content for noncommercial purposes when you:

2. Link to <https://github.com/NATroutter/BaudBound>.
3. Link to the CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 license.
4. State whether you changed the content.
5. Publish adaptations under the same license.

The BaudBound name, logos, word marks, and other distinctive brand identifiers are owned by NATroutter and excluded from the software and content licenses. The [brand notice](https://github.com/NATroutter/BaudBound/blob/master/TRADEMARKS.md) permits accurate references and attribution but does not permit branding a modified distribution as BaudBound or implying project endorsement.

## Contributions

By intentionally submitting a contribution, you agree to license it under the terms that apply to the part of the repository you changed. Code contributions use PolyForm Noncommercial 1.0.0. Documentation and non-code creative contributions use CC BY-NC-SA 4.0.

Only submit work that you have the right to license. Identify any third-party material and preserve its copyright and license notices. A dependency or third-party asset keeps its own license even when distributed with BaudBound.

## Earlier releases

Changing the repository license does not withdraw permissions already granted for copies published under an earlier license. Check the license files included with the specific version you received.
